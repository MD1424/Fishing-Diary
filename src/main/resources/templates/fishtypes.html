Fish Types

There are many different types of fish that you can target

Bass: The most famous of a all of game fish but somtimes the hardest to target. You will have to learn how to follow their bedding patterns to sucessfully catch the big one!

Walleye: Another great catch in many lakes! They like deep spots near rocks so be sure to check your depth finder if you are looking for these monsters!

Northerns: Tough fighters but can be caught with most baits as they are very aggressive and territorial. They put up a fun fight but beware of the teeth!

Pan-Fish: Sunnies and crappies are easier to catch! They will bit at anything you put in front of them but if you want to catch a good sized one you will have to find the right spot and the right lure.

Cat-fish: Bottom feeders and very heavy, hard fighters so make sure you have the proper line weight that is needed for the size of cat-fish that youa re going for.
